Main Characters

Eragon Shadeslayer

Eragon is the protagonist of the series, a young farm boy who discovers his destiny as a Dragon Rider. His journey begins when he finds a mysterious dragon egg, leading him to become a key figure in the fight against the tyrannical king, Galbatorix.

Saphira

Saphira is Eragon's dragon companion, who hatches from the egg he finds. She is a powerful and intelligent dragon, and her bond with Eragon is central to their quest to free Alagaësia from oppression.

Arya

Arya is an elven princess and a skilled warrior. She plays a crucial role in assisting Eragon and Saphira, and her knowledge and abilities are vital to their success in the struggle against Galbatorix.
